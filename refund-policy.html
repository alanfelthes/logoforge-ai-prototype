Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: · Version 1.0

This policy explains when Service Credits, logo generation charges and payments initiated through our registered business websites may be refunded or canceled.

1. Scope

This policy covers LogoForge Service Credits and payments initiated by any registered website owned and operated by the same legal entity identified in our Terms. The central checkout, payment receipt and card statement identify the same merchant of record. A source website may apply product-specific cancellation conditions, but they must be consistent with this policy and mandatory consumer law.

2. Unused Service Credits

A customer may request a refund of unused purchased Service Credits within 14 days of purchase, unless a longer mandatory cancellation period applies. Credits already consumed by completed generations are not “unused.” Promotional or bonus credits are not cash-refundable. Refunds return to the original payment method where possible.

3. Failed or duplicate transactions

Duplicate card charges, duplicate Service Credit purchases and verified technical payment errors are refundable after review. A failed logo generation that produces no usable delivered asset should automatically release the reservation or reverse the related Service Credit charge.

4. Delivered generations

Because creative preference is subjective and provider costs are incurred immediately, a delivered generation is not automatically refundable merely because the customer dislikes the style. We may offer correction, regeneration or a goodwill credit. This rule does not limit statutory rights for defective, misdescribed or undelivered digital services.

5. Cancellation

A logo generation may be canceled without charge before the provider request begins. Once processing has started, cancellation may not be technically possible. Orders initiated by another registered business website follow the product-specific cancellation terms displayed on that website and at checkout, while the same legal entity remains responsible for processing the payment and refund.

6. How to request a refund

Contact with the account email, payment reference, date, amount and reason. We may request additional evidence to prevent fraud. We aim to acknowledge requests promptly; insert the actual service-level commitment used by the business.

7. Processing time

Approved refunds are submitted to the payment provider promptly. The customer’s bank determines when funds appear, commonly several business days after submission. Do not promise a fixed bank settlement time unless verified for the payment method.

8. Chargebacks

Customers should contact support before filing a payment dispute so we can investigate. This does not waive any legal right to dispute an unauthorized or incorrect charge.

9. Mandatory rights

Nothing in this policy removes consumer rights that cannot legally be waived. Have counsel adapt digital-content cancellation language for every market served.
