AI Output & Intellectual Property Policy

Last updated: · Version 1.0

This policy explains the limitations, permitted use and review responsibilities associated with AI-generated logo concepts.

1. AI-assisted service

LogoForge uses third-party generative AI models to create visual concepts. A generated image is a starting point for branding work, not a legal clearance report or guarantee of originality.

2. Similarity and uniqueness

Models can create outputs that are similar to existing designs or to outputs generated for other users. We do not offer exclusive model training or exclusivity over visual concepts unless separately agreed in writing.

3. Trademark and legal clearance

Before using a logo commercially, conduct trademark, company-name, domain and design searches in relevant countries and classes. Consult qualified intellectual-property counsel. LogoForge does not register trademarks or guarantee registrability.

4. Rights in output

As between you and LogoForge, and subject to applicable law and relevant third-party provider terms, you may use outputs delivered to your account for lawful purposes. Copyright status for AI-generated material differs by jurisdiction and may depend on human creative contribution. No representation is made that an output receives copyright protection.

5. Human review

Check spelling, geometry, cultural meaning, accessibility, unintended symbols and resemblance to existing brands. Obtain manual vector refinement before high-value commercial rollout.

6. Inputs and references

Only upload references you have the right to use. Do not request direct imitation of living artists, protected brands or characters in a way that infringes rights or misleads the public.

7. Provider changes

AI providers, model capabilities, moderation rules and pricing can change. LogoForge maintains versioned provider and pricing records for audit and billing.
