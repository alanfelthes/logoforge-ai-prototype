Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: · Version 1.0

This policy protects customers, payment providers, AI providers and third parties from abusive or unlawful use.

1. General rule

Use LogoForge only for lawful, authorized brand-design activity. You are responsible for the content you submit and the way you use generated output.

2. Prohibited content and conduct

  • Requests to copy, closely imitate or confuse customers with an existing trademark, company logo or protected character.
  • Fraud, impersonation, phishing, deceptive businesses, counterfeit goods or misleading official seals.
  • Illegal products or services, exploitation, abuse, hateful content, violent extremist promotion or non-consensual intimate content.
  • Personal data, confidential material or regulated information submitted without authority.
  • Malware, credential theft, attempts to bypass moderation, automated abuse, scraping or attacks on the service.
  • Payment fraud, card testing, money laundering, unauthorized reselling of payment services or manipulation of refunds and chargebacks.
  • Use that violates Stripe, OpenAI or other provider policies applicable to the request.

3. Enforcement

We may block a request, remove content, restrict payments, preserve evidence, suspend an account or report activity where required. Enforcement decisions may use automated signals and human review.

4. Appeals

Contact with the decision reference and relevant context. Appeals do not require us to restore content that would violate law or provider rules.
