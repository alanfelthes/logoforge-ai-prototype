Service credits

Multi-site payments

Every registered website is operated by the same legal entity and uses the same Stripe merchant account, while reports remain separated by site.

6

Receipts and support

Every payment and generation has a reference, receipt, support path and documented refund decision.

Usage-based pricing without surprises

Charges are based on measured OpenAI image-generation usage, not a hidden flat fee.

Customer charge = provider cost × 5

Before generation, LogoForge reserves a conservative maximum. After delivery, the platform settles exactly five times the current provider cost recorded for that generation and releases the unused reserve. Taxes, when legally required, are displayed separately at checkout.
